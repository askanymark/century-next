ABOUT US

Our story goes back 30 years ago, where founding partners have engaged into petroleum products trading activity, and have eventually established themselves as one of the largest physical traders in the Baltics region, while also diversifying the business activity by involvement in the financial markets. Over time the partners' focus has shifted to private equity and venture capital, which has lead to formation of the Century Next holding.

Over the long history of operations the management has established strong personal relations with numerous international partners, including first class banks, brokerage firms, trading companies and others, and now aims to use gained experience and expertise in the private equity, and venture capital markets. Our highly flexible and knowledgeable team takes a pro-active investment approach with a view to achieve maximum results. We hope to establish strong long term relations and are always ready for the next opportunity which may arise.
